Our Story

Founded in 2009, Sesmine Oman began as a vision to transform the mining and mineral processing landscape in the Sultanate of Oman. What started as a small engineering consultancy has grown into a comprehensive solutions provider, serving clients across the Middle East and beyond.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships with global leaders, and an unwavering commitment to delivering excellence in every project. Today, we stand as a trusted partner for mining companies, offering end-to-end solutions from initial feasibility studies to complete plant commissioning.

With deep roots in Omani soil and a global perspective, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities in the region's mining sector. Our success is built on the foundation of technical expertise, local knowledge, and international best practices.
