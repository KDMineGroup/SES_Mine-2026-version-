About SESMINE

Oman's trusted partner in mining equipment solutions for over two decades

Our Story
Equipment Excellence Since 2003

Founded in 2003, SESMINE has grown from a small equipment supplier to become Oman's most trusted mining equipment solutions provider. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide mining companies with the highest quality equipment, genuine spare parts, and unmatched technical support.

Over the past two decades, we have built strong partnerships with global equipment manufacturers including Caterpillar, Komatsu, Liebherr, Sandvik, and Volvo Construction Equipment. This allows us to offer our clients access to the world's best mining equipment and technology.

Today, SESMINE operates from our state-of-the-art facility in Muscat Industrial Area, housing over 15,000 genuine spare parts and serving mining operations across the Sultanate of Oman.
