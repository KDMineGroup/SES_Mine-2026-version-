Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?

Click on the "New Project" button in the dashboard or projects section, fill in the required details, and save.

How do I use the cost calculator?

Navigate to Tools section, select Cost Calculator, and follow the step-by-step wizard to input your project parameters.

Can I export my reports?

Yes! All reports can be exported as PDF or Excel files from the report generation tool.
