Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges accordingly.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes, you save 20% when you choose annual billing. For example, Starter is $39/month (billed annually) instead of $49/month.

What happens when I exceed my project limit?

We'll notify you when approaching your limit. You can upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional project credits as needed.
