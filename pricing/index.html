Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all features of your chosen plan. No credit card required. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges or credits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), wire transfers, and purchase orders for Enterprise and Custom plans.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly billing. That's like getting 2.4 months free every year.

Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for educational institutions and registered non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your projects and reports during this period. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.
